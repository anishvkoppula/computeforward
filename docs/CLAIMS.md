## Publication rule

Every new quantitative or outcome claim must record:

1. Exact wording.
2. Metric definition and denominator.
3. Cohort and reporting period.
4. Source location and accountable owner.
5. Small-sample and attribution risks.
6. Student/guardian consent where a story or identity is involved.
7. Approval date and review/expiration date.
